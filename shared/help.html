Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a hackathon?
To register for a hackathon, navigate to the event page and click the "Register" button. You'll need to fill out your basic information, select your experience level, and indicate whether you're looking for team members or already have a complete team. Make sure to read the event requirements and terms before submitting your registration.
Can I participate in multiple hackathons simultaneously?
Yes, you can register for multiple hackathons, but we recommend focusing on one at a time for the best experience. Concurrent participation in multiple events may affect the quality of your submissions and team collaboration. Check the dates and ensure you can commit adequate time to each event.
How does the AI team finder work?
Our AI team finder analyzes your skills, interests, experience level, and project preferences to match you with compatible teammates. The algorithm considers complementary skill sets, time zone compatibility, and similar project goals. You can review suggested matches and send connection requests to potential team members.
What are the submission requirements for projects?
Project submissions typically require a working prototype or demo, source code repository, project documentation, and a presentation or pitch deck. Specific requirements vary by hackathon, so check the event guidelines. Most hackathons also require a demo video showcasing your solution's key features and functionality.
How are projects judged and winners selected?
Projects are evaluated by expert judges based on criteria such as innovation, technical implementation, design and user experience, business potential, and presentation quality. Each criterion is weighted differently depending on the hackathon focus. Judges review submitted materials and may conduct live presentations or demonstrations.
Can I change my team after registration?
Yes, team changes are allowed until the team formation deadline specified in each hackathon. You can add or remove team members through your team dashboard. However, all team members must be registered participants, and teams cannot exceed the maximum size limit (usually 4-5 members).
