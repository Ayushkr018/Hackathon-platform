About the Challenge

The Web3 Innovation Challenge 2025 is the premier blockchain hackathon bringing together the brightest minds in decentralized technology. Over 72 hours, teams will compete to build groundbreaking solutions in DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, and Web3 infrastructure.


This isn't just a competition—it's a launchpad for the next generation of blockchain applications. With expert mentorship, cutting-edge tools, and a $50,000 prize pool, participants will have everything they need to turn their ideas into reality.


Whether you're a seasoned blockchain developer or new to Web3, this challenge offers the perfect environment to learn, build, and connect with like-minded innovators from around the globe.

Challenge Tracks

DeFi Innovation

Build next-generation decentralized finance protocols, yield farming platforms, automated trading systems, or cross-chain DeFi solutions.

NFT & Digital Assets

Create innovative NFT marketplaces, digital art platforms, gaming assets, or utility-focused NFT applications with real-world use cases.

DAO & Governance

Develop decentralized autonomous organizations, governance platforms, voting systems, or community management tools for Web3.

Infrastructure & Tooling

Build developer tools, blockchain infrastructure, scaling solutions, or interoperability protocols that advance the Web3 ecosystem.
